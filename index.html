The Mathematics society

The Mathematics Society is amongest the oldest and biggest departmental Society in College. The Society is devoted to pursuing activities aimed at fostering interest in and creative expression of Mathematical Ideas, Both in college and outside it. We are one the few, if not the only society in DU to boast of two-major national events, a Mathematical Journal, A nation-wide workshop series and several other events..
