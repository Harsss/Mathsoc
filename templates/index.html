About Us

We are a bunch of individuals who, even if do not love maths, are striving hard to make it fun in any way we can.

We aim to create a healthy competetive space among the competitors and build and enhance qualities such as leadership and management among members

We have been one of the most active society in the DU Circuit, with our events registering greatest footfalls.

Events

Bezerka

Bezerka

The fun puzzle event brings out the fun side of subject as well as logical skills of the participatnts. This is our first online event where we get more than 500 registrations.

Mathopolis

Mathopolis

A quiz everything about maths. It is a battle of brain for quizzers and an opportunity for all quizzers. It is a feast for all who want to learn something new from new and broaden perspective.

Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt is basically a series of clues, one leading to the other, with the fastest to solve all clues winning the T-Hunt. It is one of our biggest events

Symposium

Symposium.

A 2 day lecture series, workshop, tutorials on topics related to maths. We have eminent professors in it form IISER, DRDO, IISC,IITs etc for sharing their knowledge with students.

Sb Mathur Lectures

Sb Mathur Lectures

An annual congregation of maths professors and enthusiastic with a great learning process going underway. It is a sneak peak for many math enthusiasts.

Integration

Integeration

The annual 2 days fest of mathematics society having the amalgation of more than 6 events and had participation from all over the university.

Event X

Alohomora

A variable event. Alohomora is a fun event based on the theme of Harry Potter. Even an online event but its feedback is mind blowing amazing.

Nagpaul Fellowship Programme

Nagpaul Fellowship Programme

A collaboration between Society an dcollege professors for work together on a research Paper. This is the great exposure for all those who had deep interest in research and want a chance to nurutre their skills.
